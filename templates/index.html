Welcome!

Here you will find three interactive analysis tools to help you decide when to schedule your flight.

The map was made on Tableau. There you can view the delay rates by state with an associated table. Click on a state or change the filters to narrow your search.
The graphs were made with plotly. There you can view similar data.
The prediction section allows you to input your flight information and get a prediction if your flight will be delayed or on time.
